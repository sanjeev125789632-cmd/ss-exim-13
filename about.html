About S S EXIM Electrical Engineering Services

S S EXIM combines manufacturing discipline and field execution experience to deliver practical electrical and automation outcomes for industrial clients.

Who We Are

We are a Mumbai-headquartered engineering company focused on electrical panel manufacturing, smart building controls, and dependable power distribution products. Our teams support consultant-led and EPC-led projects where performance, documentation quality, and timeline control are non-negotiable. With offices in Thane, Pune, and Bangalore, we coordinate local site response while maintaining centralized quality standards and procurement governance.

Our service model covers design inputs, fabrication support, supply coordination, and commissioning assistance for commercial and industrial facilities.
